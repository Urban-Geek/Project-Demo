🎓 Scholarships & Funding for Students

February's Recommendations — amazing scholarship opportunities for students with disabilities and their families!

💛

Anne Ford Scholarship

A $10,000 scholarship ($2,500/year over 4 years) for graduating high school seniors with a documented learning disability (LD). Students with ADHD who also have an LD are encouraged to apply. Must enroll in a full-time bachelor's degree program.

📅 Rolling Deadline: April 26, 2026
Apply Now ✨
🎰

NM Lottery Scholarship

New Mexico's first tuition-free scholarship program — covers 100% of tuition for recent high school graduates. A great opportunity for students heading straight to college!
